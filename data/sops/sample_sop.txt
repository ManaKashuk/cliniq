Purpose: Example SOP excerpt for demonstration only.
Scope: Site-wide.

Adverse Event (AE) Documentation:
1. Record AE in subject source with date/time, description, and severity per protocol.
2. Notify PI within required timeframe; document notification.
3. Complete AE form; cross-reference subject ID (no PHI in this file).
4. File documents in regulatory binder per indexing rules.

Delegation Log Management:
1. Maintain current delegation log with role, task scope, start/end dates.
2. PI approval required prior to task assignment.
3. Update log upon staff onboarding/offboarding; retain previous versions.
